Optimization Laboratory

メッセージ　Message

拓殖大学 工学部 情報工学科 澄川研究室にようこそ！澄川研では、「最適化」をキーワードに、プログラミング言語処理系、機械学習と自然言語処理の歴史学や教育工学への応用、ソフトウェア工学の研究を行っています。

Welcome to Sumikawa Laboratory, Department of Computer Science, Faculty of Engineering, Takushoku University! Our research interests include programming language and its implementation, application of machine learning and natural language processing to history and educational technology, and software engineering. In our laboratory, we are focusing on eliminating various wastes in systems and human activities with optimization as the keyword in these research areas.
